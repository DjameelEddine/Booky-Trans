Frequently Asked Question

How can I become a volunteer translator?
Simply sign up for a free account, browse our library of books waiting for translation, and choose a book in a language you're fluent in. You can start translating immediately and contribute to making knowledge accessible across languages. No formal certification requiredâ€”just passion and language skills!
I found an error in a translation, what should I do?
You can report errors by leaving a review on the translation page. Our community reviews help maintain quality and allow translators to improve their work. If you're confident in the correction, you can also upload your own improved version of the translation.
Is there a way to request a specific book to be translated?
Yes! You can upload the book yourself to make it available for translation by our community. Simply go to the "Upload a Book" section, provide the book details and original file, and translators worldwide will be able to see it and start working on translations.
Can I download translated books for free?
Absolutely! All translations on BookyTrans are freely available to download. Our mission is to break down language barriers and make literature accessible to everyone, everywhere. Just browse, find your book, and download in your preferred language.
What file formats do you support for uploads?
We currently support PDF, EPUB, and TXT formats for both book uploads and translations. These formats ensure compatibility across different devices and make it easy for translators to work with the content.
